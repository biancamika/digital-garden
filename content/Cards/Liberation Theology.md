## Notes
**Liberation theology** is a [Christian theological](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_theological "Christian theological") approach emphasizing the liberation of the oppressed. It engages in [socio-economic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socio-economic "Socio-economic") analyses, with social concern for the poor and political liberation for oppressed peoples[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberation_theology#cite_note-1) and addresses other forms of inequality, such as race or caste.
